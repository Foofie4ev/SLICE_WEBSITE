TENNIS WITH A TWIST!

The card game that blends a Tennis match into a fresh simple yet strategic card game! Serve it! Slice it! Spin it! Dice it! Win it!

GAMEPLAY

Fast Paced

Rallies move fast! With the unique "Slice" mechanic, momentum can shift in a single card drop!

Risk & Reward

Roll the dice on a Volley or Drop Shot! Will you hit a winner or dump it into the net?!

Real Tactics

Cross-court, down-the-line, Lobs, and Smashes. It's a simulation hidden inside a fast paced card game!

Singles & Doubles

Play head-to-head in intense 1v1 matches or team up for strategic 2v2 doubles action!

THE RECIPE FOR
VICTORY!

1

Served Fresh!

Start the point by rolling the Serve Dice. Your roll sets the initial Pace Value.

1st Serve

2nd Serve

2

Delicious Rallies!

Play Cross-Court (same Shot Type, higher Pace) or Down-the-Line (switch Shot Type, equal Pace).

3

Slice It Up!

Play a SLICE card to reverse the momentum. Opponent must play a LOWER Pace card. It's a rhythm breaker!

4

Risk it All!

Attack the net with a risky Volley or Drop-Shot card and roll the dice for your Pace, just don't hit it out!

Drop-Shot

Volley

5

Smash it Down!

Punish a weak shot with a Smash card or defend against an aggressive attack with a Lob card.

6

Highs and Lows!

Play a Winner to win a critical rally and avoid playing Error cards during big match moments!
