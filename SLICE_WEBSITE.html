TENNIS WITH A TWIST!

The card game that blends a tennis match into a fresh 1 vs 1 and 2 v2 strategic card game! Outwit, outlast, and with a little luck, out-juice your opponent!

Fast Paced

Rallies move fast. With the unique "Slice" mechanic, momentum can shift in a single card drop.

Risk & Reward

Roll the dice on a Volley or Drop Shot. Will you hit a winner or smash it into the net?

Real Tactics

Cross-court, down-the-line, lobs, and smashes. It's a simulation hidden inside a fast paced card game.

The Rally

Play Cross-Court (same shot type, higher pace) or Down-the-Line (switch shot type, equal pace).

Slice & Dice

Play a SLICE card to reverse the momentum. Opponent must play a LOWER pace card. It's a rhythm breaker.

4

Attack the Net

Risk it all by playing a Volley or Drop-Shot and roll the dice for your Pace, just dont hit it out!
